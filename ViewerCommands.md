## Viewer Commands

### #points

Shows your current points. Gain points by watching stream, cheering bits, subscribing, or gifting subs.


### Controls

| Command | Description | Cost |
|---------|-------------|------|
| `#launch` | Launch Player | 50 |
| `#nofasttravel` | Disable Fast Travel | 100 |
| `#nojumping` | Disable Sprinting and Jumping | 100 |
| `#nomenus` | Disable VATS and PipBoy | 100 |
| `#save` | Auto Save | 10 |
